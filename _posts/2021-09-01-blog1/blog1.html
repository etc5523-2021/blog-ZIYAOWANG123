Target Country - United Stated

  • Brief Introduction: It has been a long time since the rapid spread of coronavirus, it creates many inconveniences for people, more importantly, threatening our physical and psychological health. While projecting ourselves safe from the infection, it is also valuable to obtain updated information to inform ourselves of the latest progress since this is a fight for all mankind. Thus, this blog will introduce an overview of the COVID-19 situation in the USA with associated details.

Data Description

This blog has used two main data sets as the base for the construction of data communication regarding COVID-19 information.

Data Source 1 - R Package nCov2019

The data set is collected from various sources for different relevant information.
- Latest data of corona virus cases from worldometers
- Historical data of corona virus cases from JHUCSSE
- Vaccine data from raps.org
- Therapeutics data from raps.org

This data source contains two core parts. The first one is the GUI explorer section which has a function of the dashboard to use a simple and interactive dashboard for information communication.

The second part is the CLI analysis which includes a workflow from data query to data operation and lastly the visualization output of both geographic and animation plots.

This blog will focus on the CLI analysis part, which will query the updated COVID-19 data from five different aspects. It will include the “latest update COVID-19 info.”, “global COVID-19 info.”, “historical COVID-19 info.”, “vaccine details”, “therapeutics status and progress”.

Data Source 2 - New York Times Covid-19 data sets

This data source is collected by the journalists from the New York Times across several time zones to monitor news conferences and public data releases with seeking clarification from official bodies. It is under the license of CC BY-NC which allows the users to adapt the data with proper attribution given.

Contact Information: covid-data@nytimes.com

Exploratory 2 - US Focus

The table 2 shows the monthly movement of USA’s daily confirmed cases, death and recoveries.

Something to notice: 1. The number of deaths is decreasing over time, observing it through checking the numbers under New Deaths.
2. Based on the daily number of confirmed new cases, the pandemic was under controlled in June and August 2020, yet quickly rebounded back from October. In May 2021, things were under control again yet not for long, another breakout started in July.

  • The possible cause for this repetitive fluctuation on the COVID-19 control in the USA may be caused by various reasons. One of them may be the US President Election, which made a change to the government body and led to different implementations against the pandemic issues.

  • Another reason may be that many civil rights protests were held during the time, which may result in a lagged rapid increase in the confirmed cases after the event. Examples - BLM

  • There is a stop of daily recoveries report at 13/12/2020, it may cause by either stop record of the data source, or miss recording.
